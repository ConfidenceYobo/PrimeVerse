PRIME MARKETPLACE

The Peer-2-Peer marketplace for digital creative assets and services will be a good place for creators and freelancers that will have the opportunity to get involved in a new form of global e-commerce based on blockchain technology.

Through the platform, we want to give an additional opportunity to generate online financial revenues.

PRIME PLAY

T-Rex apocalypse is our Play-to-Earn game! It uses true cinematic quality 3D NFTs in every area of the gameplay. It is required for every player to hold 666,666,666 $Prime to be able access the game. This will further enhance the adoption of PrimeVerse Token and consequently keep its value rising.

PRIME NFT COLLECTION

The Prime NFT collection will be built on the Binance Smart Chain and all designs will be inspired by the characters and items used in the PrimePlay P2E game. The collection will contain 100 pieces of NFTs, each NFT with its own uniqueness, rarity etc.

PRIME HEART

This is a charity program we have designed as a means to give back to humanity. Funds are taken from Marketing and Development wallet, to be able to make this charity program work. We aim to give to those who are less privileged in the society by providing such people with Food supplies, Medical care, clothing and finances. We will be holding charity events in different countries of the world and this process will be very transparent to the community members, as they will contribute immensely to the decision making regarding all charity processes.
